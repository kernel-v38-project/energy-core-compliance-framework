Core Architectural Blueprint

The platform is organized around four enterprise layers:

Field Telemetry

Read-only ingestion from physical SCADA/PLC systems through secure edge gateways.

Copernicus Engine

ETL pipelines enriching telemetry with European Copernicus satellite weather data.

Audit Trail

Workflow and revenue traceability backed by blockchain-aligned notarization.

Operations UI

High-performance multipage executive interface for multi-site analytics.
