# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) Summary

**Policy Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-25

## Purpose

This document is an operational summary of data processing terms for energy_core and must be aligned with customer-specific contractual terms.

## Roles of the Parties

Typically:

- the customer acts as controller;
- energy_core acts as processor or service provider as defined by contract;
- subprocessors support service delivery where applicable.

## Processing Instructions

energy_core processes personal data only:

- to deliver the service;
- under documented customer instructions;
- to meet security, support, and compliance duties;
- to maintain platform operations within contractual limits.

## Categories of Data

Categories may include account, contact, authorization, security log, telemetry, audit, and customer-provided data.

## Security Measures

Security measures include tenant isolation, role-based access controls, strong authentication, logging/auditability, encryption in transit, backup and restore validation, and incident response controls.

## Subprocessors

Subprocessors are used only where required for service delivery and must align with the current list in [docs/subprocessors.md](./docs/subprocessors.md), which is indexed in the Trust Center.

## Confidentiality

Authorized personnel with data access must be subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

## Data Subject Requests

energy_core supports customers with data subject request handling within service scope.

## Retention and Deletion

Upon service termination or documented customer instruction, data should be returned, exported, or deleted according to contract, except where legal retention obligations apply.

## Breach Notification

Significant security incidents follow documented detection, containment, analysis, customer communication, remediation, and post-incident review.

## Audit and Cooperation

energy_core may support reasonable audits, questionnaire responses, control documentation, and evidence sharing aligned with service scope.
