# Commercial Model

## Overview

The commercial structure is designed to support multiple enterprise monetization paths.

## Revenue Models

### 1. Enterprise Licensing
Annual licensing based on:
- Number of sites
- Data throughput
- Feature modules enabled

### 2. Platform Licensing
White-label deployment for:
- Utility providers
- Industrial operators
- Infrastructure integrators

### 3. Technology Transfer
Full or partial IP acquisition agreements under M&A structure.

### 4. Strategic Partnerships
Joint deployment with:
- Energy infrastructure companies
- Cloud providers
- Industrial automation vendors

## Pricing Logic (Conceptual)

Pricing is typically structured around:
- Operational scale
- Data volume
- Compliance requirements
- Integration complexity

## Delivery Model

- Cloud deployment
- Hybrid industrial deployments
- On-premise deployments for regulated environments
