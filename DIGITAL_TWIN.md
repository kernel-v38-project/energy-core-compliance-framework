# Digital Twin Framework

## Overview

Energy_Core Enterprise supports the conceptual foundation for digital twin modeling of energy infrastructure assets.

## Twin Structure

Each asset may be represented as:

- Physical state (telemetry)
- Environmental context (external conditions)
- Operational state (system behavior)
- Historical state (time-series evolution)

## Purpose

To enable:

- Asset-level intelligence
- Cross-site comparison
- Environmental impact correlation
- Operational optimization frameworks

## Design Principle

The digital twin model is not a simulation layer, but a structured representation of real-world operational data enriched with environmental context.
