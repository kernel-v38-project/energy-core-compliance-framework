# Privacy Overview

## Data Philosophy

Energy_Core Enterprise is designed to process industrial and operational data, not personal consumer data.

## Data Categories

- Industrial telemetry data
- Environmental datasets
- Operational metadata
- System audit logs

## Personal Data Handling

The platform is not designed for personal data processing. If personal data is incidentally present in operational datasets, it is treated as non-primary data and subject to minimization principles.

## Data Usage

Data is used exclusively for:

- Operational intelligence
- Validation processes
- Environmental enrichment
- Audit and compliance reporting

## Data Retention

Retention policies are configurable at deployment level and depend on:
- Client requirements
- Regulatory environment
- Operational constraints

## Data Access

Access is strictly controlled via:
- Role-based permissions
- Audit logging
- Segmented system access
